IoT Device Simulator

virtual device simulation solution to evaluate IoT applications

The IoT Device Simulator helps you easily build a pool of virtual devices to evaluate your IoT applications that gather, process, analyze and act on data generated by connected devices.

Benefits and Features

Experiment iteratively and quickly

Experiment iteratively and quickly to build intelligent, powerful IoT applications without the initial overhead of device integration or maintaining a large pool of devices.

Easy to use interface

Provides the ability to easily launch hundreds of IoT widgets created from a variety of device templates you define all within the comforts of a web-based GUI.

Monitor and observe

Once your devices have started simulating data, you can monitor individual devices from the console or observe how your backend services are processing the data being published by the simulator.

Use it out-of-the-box or customize it

The solution runs within a your own account and can be used out-of-the-box, or used as a platform for building a customized simulation engine to meet a your unique business requirements.
